RM (Kim Namjoon)

RM, once Rap Monster, is the leader of the group and one of the rappers of the group.He was the first one recruited for the group and only one who can speak English fluently, thus he is usually the one who does the talking in English interviews. When BTS is writing their own songs, usually the producers take RM's writing into the songs. His IQ is said to be on the genius level. Although he is the leader, he is not the oldest member. He was born on September 12, 1994, has a dog name Rapmon, and is the clumsiest member of thr group. He has been seen in videos breaking glasses he just bought, beds, the glass floor from dancing, and even simply standing there but the bag he was carrying ripped. RM used to be called Rap Monster, but he felt as he got older he wanted a more professional name so he initialized it, into RM. RM also has his own songs such as Change with rapper Wale.
