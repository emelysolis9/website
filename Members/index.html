RM (Kim Namjoon)

RM, once Rap Monster, is the leader of the group and one of the rappers of the group.He was the first one recruited for the group and only one who can speak English fluently, thus he is usually the one who does the talking in English interviews. When BTS is writing their own songs, usually the producers take RM's writing into the songs. His IQ is said to be on the genius level. Although he is the leader, he is not the oldest member. He was born on September 12, 1994, has a dog name Rapmon, and is the clumsiest member of thr group. He has been seen in videos breaking glasses he just bought, beds, the glass floor from dancing, and even simply standing there but the bag he was carrying ripped. RM used to be called Rap Monster, but he felt as he got older he wanted a more professional name so he initialized it, into RM. RM also has his own songs such as Change with rapper Wale. RM is highly respected by his other members and although may have a strong apperance, he is very sweet.

Suga (Min Yoongi)

Suga is another rapper in the group and got his name because when he smiles he looks sweet like sugar. Suga was born on March 9th, 1993, but is jokingly called the child of the group by members, but grandpa by ARMYs. He is very passionate about music. Before BTS, he was very poor and it also affected his mental health, He gravely suffered depression as he speaks about in his mixtape Agust D, song Last of Me. He dreamt of going far with music that he would have to choose between either spening money on food or transportation to take him to places that will help him continue his music. This has caused him to go nights and days without eating. Now he spends hours in his own little studio, working on music and song writing. Suga although in pictures he looks sweet, he is also considered the "savage" of the group, making sarcastic remarks and making faces when members act silly or extra.
